Breads

White Bread

Ingredients

  • 11 c. flour
  • 2 T. yeast
  • 6 T. sugar
  • 6 T. oil
  • 4T salt
  • 1 T. milk
  • 3 c. water
  • Equipment: kettle, large mixing bowl, large mixing spoon (wooden), 4 cup glass measuring cup, plastic wrap, dish towel

Steps

  1. Put water in a kettle and turn on to heat up.
  2. Put 4 cups of flour and all other dry ingredients into a very large bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Pour oil and milk into large glass measuring cup. If you don’t have a large measuring cup, combine ingredients in a medium bowl.
  4. Start adding the 3 cups of hot water. When you only have about a half a cup left, feel the water and keep stirring with the spoon.
  5. Mix the liquids into the flour with a large spoon. I use a wooden spoon.
  6. Pour remaining flour into the bowl, stirring as you go along. When it’s too hard to stir, add flour onto your hands and mix with your hands.
  7. Stop adding flour or add more flour to get a consistency where it’s no longer sticking to your fingers.
  8. Knead about ten minutes. You can’t overknead when you are doing it by hand. You lift the bread onto its end, push it down and forward, and then turn it.
  9. Stop when it’s satiny and it springs back right away when you press on it.
  10. Pour oil around the edge of the bowl. Spin the dough around in the oil to cover the dough and the bowl with oil.
  11. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and then a towel.
  12. If you are in a hurry, put it in a warm place.
  13. After it has doubled in size, punch it down and form it how you’d like to cook it: loaf, bread sticks, rolls, etc.
  14. Let it rise another 40 minutes before baking at 400 degree in the oven for 20 minutes.

Soups

Red Lentil Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 1/2 c. red lentils
  • 1 T. Vegeta
  • Salt and pepper as desired

Equipment: soup pot, stirring spoon, sharp knife, grater

Steps

  1. Grate two carrots.
  2. Finely dice a medium onion.
  3. Saute the onion in a splotch of oil in the pot. Traditionally you'd add a tablespoon or two of tomato paste at this point.
  4. Add 7 cups of water, the carrots, and lentils.
  5. Season with Vegeta.
  6. Bring to a boil and then turn to the lowest simmer.
  7. It will take about an hour for the lentils to cook.

Notes: You can let it cook for as long as you like, but keep it on very low heat and stir whenever you walk past.
